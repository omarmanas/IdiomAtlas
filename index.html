About the Project

Idioms Atlas compares how Turkish, English, and Lithuanian express similar human experiences in different ways.

Each idiom is explored through meaning, metaphor, grammar notes, cultural nuance, example sentences, and short human stories.

Sometimes languages share the same image. Sometimes they see the world in a completely different way. Both are valuable. Both are human.

About the Author

Omer Manas is a self-published author and language enthusiast with a deep interest in cultural expression, translation, and the beauty of human communication.

After living in Lithuania for several years and learning Lithuanian as a foreigner, he became fascinated by how different languages shape the way we think, feel, and speak.
